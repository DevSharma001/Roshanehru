ABOUT COACH

Train smart, build strength, and unlock skills — step by step.

Roshan Nehru is a calisthenics athlete and coach with 10+ years of training experience. He’s a 2x National Champion and the World Battle of the Year 2023 winner, and the founder of RawStrength Calisthenics.

Whether you’re starting from zero or chasing advanced skills, coaching is built around clean form, progressive overload, mobility, and consistency—so you get stronger without getting injured.
