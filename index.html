ABOUT THE COACH

Roshan Nehru — Calisthenics Athlete & Coach

I’m Roshan Nehru — Founder of RawStrength Calisthenics, 2x National Champion, and World Battle of the Year 2023 Winner. I’ve spent years mastering bodyweight strength, dynamics, and skill work with one mission: help you build a powerful body using the right technique and smart progression.

Whether you’re a complete beginner or an athlete chasing advanced skills, my coaching focuses on form-first training, progressive overload, and sustainable results — so you don’t just “workout”, you actually level up.
